## 📋 Irrigation Schedule Quick View

### Rice (वरि / चावल)
- **Duration:** 120 days
- **Critical Stages:** Panicle Initiation (40-60 days), Flowering (60-90 days)
- **Water Requirement:** 1200-1500mm annually

### Wheat (గోధుమ / गेहूं)
- **Duration:** 120 days
- **Critical Stages:** Flowering (80-85 days), Milk Stage (100-105 days)
- **Irrigations:** 4-6 times during season

### Cotton (పత్తి / कपास)
- **Duration:** 180 days
- **Critical Stages:** Flowering (60-90 days), Boll Formation (90-120 days)
- **Water Requirement:** 700-1300mm

### Maize (మొక్కజొన్న / मक्का)
- **Duration:** 100 days
- **Critical Stages:** Tasseling (45-50 days), Silking (55-60 days)
- **Method:** Furrow or sprinkler

### Sugarcane (చెరకు / गन्ना)
- **Duration:** 365 days
- **Critical Stage:** Grand Growth (120-270 days)
- **Water Requirement:** 2000-2500mm annually

### Groundnut (వేరుశెనగ / मूंगफली)
- **Duration:** 120 days
- **Critical Stages:** Flowering (30-50 days), Pegging (50-70 days)
- **Water:** Moderate requirement

### Tomato (టమాటో / टमाटर)
- **Duration:** 120 days
- **Critical Stage:** Fruit Setting (50-70 days)
- **Method:** Drip irrigation recommended

### Potato (బంగాళాదుంప / आलू)
- **Duration:** 100 days
- **Critical Stages:** Tuber Initiation (30-50 days), Tuber Bulking (50-80 days)
- **Method:** Sprinkler or drip

## 🌡️ Soil pH Quick Guide

| pH Range | Type | Best Crops |
|----------|------|------------|
| 3.5-5.5 | Strongly Acidic | Tea, Potato |
| 5.5-6.5 | Slightly Acidic | Rice, Wheat, Maize |
| 6.5-7.5 | Neutral | Most crops |
| 7.5-8.5 | Slightly Alkaline | Cotton, Sugarcane |

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Voice Not Working
1. Check microphone permission
2. Use Chrome or Edge browser
3. Speak clearly
4. Select correct language first

### Auto-Detect Not Working
1. Select Climate first
2. Select Soil Type first
3. Then click Auto Detect buttons

### No Recommendation
1. Fill all required fields
2. Check pH range (3-10)
3. Check NPK range (0-100)
4. Ensure logged in
