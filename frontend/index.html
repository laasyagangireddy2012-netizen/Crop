🧪 Soil Analysis

Understand your soil composition and health

🟫

Black Soil

Rich in clay minerals. Ideal for cotton, soybean, and wheat. pH 7.5–8.5. Found in Deccan plateau.

🟥

Red Soil

Rich in iron oxides. Suitable for groundnut, millets, and tobacco. pH 5.5–7.0. Found in peninsular India.

🟨

Sandy Soil

Low water retention. Good for watermelon, peanuts, and carrots. pH 5.5–7.0. Requires frequent irrigation.

🟤

Clay Soil

High water retention. Suitable for rice and wheat. pH 6.0–7.5. Needs good drainage management.

🌿

Loamy Soil

Best all-round soil. Ideal for most crops. pH 6.0–7.0. Balanced sand, silt, and clay composition.

💧

Alluvial Soil

Highly fertile. Excellent for rice, wheat, and sugarcane. pH 6.5–8.0. Found in river plains.
